# Levels

Documentation about what Levels are and what they mean.

## General

The Level value determines what items are unlocked during the game. The Level increases as the combined points of both teams reaches certain thresholds.

## Level thresholds

Total points required to reach each Level.

1. 0
2. 50
3. 200
4. 400
5. 600
6. 750
7. 850
8. 950
9. 1000
